READ MORE ABOUT US

AgricFi is an innovative blockchain-powered platform designed to bridge the gap between traditional agriculture and decentralized finance (DeFi). By integrating smart contracts, tokenized assets, and transparent supply chain management, AgricFi empowers farmers, investors, and stakeholders to create a more efficient, transparent, and profitable agricultural ecosystem.

The global agricultural industry generates over $10 trillion annually and supports billions of people. Yet, many farmers face challenges accessing capital, fair market pricing, and modern financial tools. AgricFi seeks to solve these issues by leveraging blockchain technology to create a trustless, secure, and inclusive ecosystem that transforms how agricultural investments and operations are managed.

Ecosystem

An Ecosystem Designed for Growth

AgricFi brings together technology, finance, and farming expertise to create a transparent, sustainable, and high-impact agricultural finance network.

Each module is built to scale trust, increase yeilds, and amplify returns for farmers and investors alike

Defi Funding Hub

The DeFi Funding Hub is the financial backbone of the AgricFi ecosystem. It bridges the funding gap between smallholder farmers and global investors by providing an open, decentralized gateway for agricultural financing.

Features
  • On-chain Verification & Escrow
  • Tokenized farm assests
  • Dynamic APYs based on performance

Sustainability Ledger

The Sustainability Ledger is AgricFi’s proof-of-impact layer. It captures and records all farm activities that contribute to environmental sustainability; ensuring transparency for investors, partners, and regulators.

Features
  • On-chain Verification & Escrow
  • Tokenized farm assests
  • Dynamic APYs based on performance

AgricFI Token Utility

The $AGFI token powers all activities within the AgricFi ecosystem. It’s the transactional, governance, and incentive engine — designed to maintain liquidity, reward contributions, and ensure decentralized control.

Features
  • On-chain Verification & Escrow
  • Tokenized farm assests
  • Dynamic APYs based on performance

Yield Dashboard

The Yield Dashboard serves as the investor’s control center — a real-time performance monitor for all active agricultural investments. It aggregates on-chain data, IoT metrics, and market analytics into a single intuitive interface, offering investors full visibility over their farm-backed assets.

Features
  • On-chain Verification & Escrow
  • Tokenized farm assests
  • Dynamic APYs based on performance

Farm NFTs

Farm NFTs transform real farmland and crop batches into verified digital assets. Each NFT represents a fraction of a real-world farm, making agricultural ownership accessible to anyone.

Features
  • Verified Asset Minting: Each NFT contains geolocation, crop details, and ownership metadata.
  • Fractional Ownership: Allows multiple investors to co-own a farm through NFT divisions.
  • Dynamic Data Integration: IoT and satellite feeds update NFT metadata automatically.

Marketplace

The Marketplace is AgricFi’s digital trading hub, where real-world farm assets meet decentralized exchange. Users can buy, sell, and auction farm NFTs, produce tokens, and sustainability credits — all within a secure on-chain marketplace.

Features
  • NFT & Produce Trading: Enables seamless buying and selling of digital farm assets.
  • Wallet Integration: Compatible with MetaMask, Trust Wallet, and other DeFi wallets.
  • Market Analytics: Real-time charts showing floor prices, trade volume, and asset performance.
