AgricFi Token (AFT)

AFT is the native token used for staking, governance, marketplace fees, and rewards. Supply is fixed and emissions are controlled.

Distribution

  • Public Sale: 25%
  • Team & Advisors: 15% (vest 12–24 months)
  • Staking Rewards: 20%
  • Liquidity Pool: 10%
  • Ecosystem Growth: 20%
  • Strategic Partnerships: 10% (locked)

Mechanics

Transaction fees partially burned. Staking tiers encourage longer lockups. NFTs and carbon credits provide additional token sinks and utility.
